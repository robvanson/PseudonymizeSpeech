Pseudomymize Speech

A Praat script to pseudonymize speech. That is, Pseudonymize Speech tries to make it difficult to recognize the speaker while still retaining relevant (para-)linguistic features and intelligibility. There is a trade-off between the level of pseudonymization and the (para-)linguistic features retained.
